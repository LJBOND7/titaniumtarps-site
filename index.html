The blue tarp problem

Blue tarps are a second disaster waiting on your roof.

On a commercial roof, a blue tarp is a 90-day product on a 12-month problem. It flaps, tears, and leaks long before the reroof starts, and every re-tarp is another mobilization, another bill, and another round of water through your tenants, inventory, and finishes. Titanium Tarps heat-seals a tensioned Elastowrap membrane to the structure, so the building stays dry and operational from the day of the loss to the last day of restoration.

Seal it once. Stay dry until the new roof is on.

Questions we get after every storm

Commercial roofs, failing tile,

Are blue tarps good enough for a commercial or industrial roof?

No. Consumer blue tarps are UV rated for roughly 90 days, they flap loose in ordinary wind, and every fastener used to hold them puts another hole in your roof. Commercial rebuilds and reroofs routinely take a year or more. We install a heat-welded Elastowrap membrane rated for 24 months, attached without a single penetration, so the building stays dry and operational.

What is shrink wrap roofing?

A heavy industrial film that is tensioned over the damaged roof, heat-shrunk into one continuous drum-tight membrane, and sealed to the structure. No seams to leak, no loose edges to catch wind. Our Elastowrap film is 12 mil thick, Class A fire rated, and manufactured in our own plant.

Can you seal a large roof while we wait on replacement tile?

Yes, this is one of the things we do most. When Ludowici, slate, clay, or custom tile is failing and the replacement is months out on a long lead time, we seal the entire roof under Elastowrap so the building stays dry and in service until the tile arrives and the permanent roof is installed. No rushed substitutions, no water damage while you wait.

Do you work on government and institutional buildings?

Yes. We secure government, municipal, military, and institutional buildings after storm and fire damage and during delayed reroofs. Our Class A fire-rated membrane and zero-penetration attachment suit occupied and secured facilities, and we document every installation for procurement and insurance.

How long does a shrink wrap roof last?

Our Elastowrap membrane carries a 24-month UV rating and has been wind tested past 150 mph at the International Hurricane Center. Most clients stay sealed from the day of the loss to the day the permanent roof is finished.

Do you work with insurance claims?

Yes. Emergency roof protection is a standard mitigation expense on most commercial property claims. We document the damage and the installation with drone photography and provide the paperwork your adjuster and board need.

How fast can you mobilize a large commercial roof?

We stage crews and material while a storm is still tracking and roll as soon as roads open. Because we manufacture the Elastowrap film ourselves, there is no waiting on back-ordered material, even for stadium and campus-scale scopes.
